About Me

Hello! I'm Elmo Juanara, a researcher and academic focusing on disaster risk reduction, geospatial analysis, and technology-driven resilience solutions. With a passion for advancing science in disaster preparedness, I work on projects related to predictive modeling and early warning systems for high-consequence events.

I hold multiple positions, including Research Assistant at Japan Advanced Institute of Science and Technology. I am also deeply involved in the academic community, contributing to international conferences and publishing peer-reviewed papers on disaster resilience and technology.

Research & Publications

My research primarily focuses on utilizing machine learning and advanced computational models to mitigate the risks associated with disasters. I am particularly interested in volcanic tsunamis and predictive systems for disaster management.
